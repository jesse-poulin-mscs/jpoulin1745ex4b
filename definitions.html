Definitions

To Fuel your Knowledge

  1. Home
  2. Definitions
Term Alias Definition
Central Processing Unit CPU Responsible for bulk of general computing tasks.
Graphics Processing Unit GPU Responsible for rendering graphics-heavy applications.
Hard Drive Disk HDD Slower than an SSD, HDDs are mostly used for high reliability and longevity in servers. Generally half the price per GB than an SSD.
Solid State Drive SSD Faster than a HDD and SSHDs, the go to option for storage of an operating system.
Solid State Hybrid Drive SSHD Combine elements of both SSDs and HDDs, usually faster than a normal HDD, but slower than a typical SSD. Priced between SSDs and HDDs.
Non-Volatile Memory express Drive NVMe Multiples of speed faster than SSDs, although current-gen PCs and OSes do not fully take advantage of the speed difference. Takes advantage of the high-speed PCIe interface. Generally the same cost of a SSD, although newer gen PCIe will cost more.
Peripheral Component Interconnect express PCIe A type of interface utilized by the graphics card, NVMe drives and other types of cards such as sound cards and adapters.
Advanced Technology eXtended ATX A popular standard size for PC motherboards and cases.
Micro ATX mATX Smaller form factor of the ATX standard.
Informational Technology eXtended ITX Smaller than the other form factors.
Personal Computer PC Term to describe a computer.
